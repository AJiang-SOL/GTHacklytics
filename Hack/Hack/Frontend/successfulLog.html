NuTree

 NuTree is initiative for the general public to be more cognizant on what they put into their bodies. Nutrients are the building blocks to human health or the foundation of life, just as the roots of a tree.

Why this?

 Our team chose this topic because keeping track of what one puts into their body can allow them to change their diet before long-term health conditions set in.
